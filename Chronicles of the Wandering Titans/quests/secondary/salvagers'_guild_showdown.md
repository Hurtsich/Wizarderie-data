# Secondary Quests

## Salvagers' Guild Showdown

**Type:** Faction (Salvagers' Guild)

**Difficulty:** Medium

**Estimated Duration:** 4-6 hours

**Description:** A valuable wreck containing precursor technology (or rare resources) has been located near Skyport Landing. The Salvagers' Guild wants it secured before the Black Wind Corsairs or a rival Guild crew arrive.

**Prerequisites:**
- Access to Skyport Landing
- Neutral or positive standing with Salvagers' Guild

### Objectives

#### Objective

Get the briefing and coordinates from the Salvagers' Guild contact in Skyport Landing.

**Success Conditions:**
- Receive mission details and location.

#### Objective

Travel quickly to the wreck location (might require sky-vessel or dangerous climb).

**Success Conditions:**
- Reach the wreck site.

**Failure Conditions:**
- Arriving too late (rivals already present)
- Getting lost

#### Objective

Locate the specific valuable cargo/component within the wreck.

**Success Conditions:**
- Target item/cargo identified.

**Failure Conditions:**
- Item is missing or destroyed
- Unable to access the item

**Alternative Outcomes:**
- Finding unexpected hazards within the wreck (e.g., automated defenses, unstable Aetherium core).

#### Objective

Extract the valuable cargo and defend the site from rivals (Corsairs or other salvagers) if they arrive.

**Success Conditions:**
- Cargo extracted.
- Rivals driven off or neutralized.
- Players secure the wreck for the Guild.

**Failure Conditions:**
- Cargo stolen by rivals.
- Players driven off or defeated.
- Wreck is destroyed during conflict.

**Alternative Outcomes:**
- Negotiating a split of the salvage with rivals.
- Sabotaging the cargo to prevent anyone from getting it.

#### Objective

Return the cargo to the Salvagers' Guild contact.

**Success Conditions:**
- Cargo delivered safely.

**Failure Conditions:**
- Cargo lost or stolen en route back.

### Additional Information

**Key NPCs:**
- Salvagers' Guild Foreman (Quest Giver, perhaps named Anya Ironhull)
- Black Wind Corsairs (Potential Antagonists)
- Rival Salvage Crew (Potential Antagonists)

**Key Locations:**
- Skyport Landing (Start/End)
- Salvagers' Guild HQ
- Wreck Site (e.g., Sky-Breaker Wreckage, on a plateau or crevice edge)

**Rewards:**
- **Primary Reward:** A significant share of the salvage value (Coin), Good standing with Salvagers' Guild.
- **Secondary Rewards:**
  - Salvaged components or materials.
  - Map of the wreck location.
- **Hidden Rewards:**
  - A functional piece of precursor tech salvaged discreetly.
  - Intel on Corsair patrol routes near Skyport Landing.
- **Reputation Impact:**
  - Anya Ironhull: Salvagers' Guild Foreman. (Faction: The Salvagers' Guild, Impact: Positive)
  - The Black Wind Corsairs: Notorious sky-pirates. (Faction: The Black Wind Corsairs, Impact: Negative (if fought))

**Branching Paths:**
- Players decide how to handle rivals: combat, negotiation, stealth.
- Players can choose to keep the most valuable item for themselves, risking Guild anger.

**Optional Content:**
- Thoroughly salvage the entire wreck after securing the main objective.
- Investigate the cause of the crash.
